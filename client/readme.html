This is a BonziBUDDY chatroom where you can hang out, talk, and goof around with your friends and that's it.
This site was made by Neil Santiago, also known as chokoto.
Released 2026.
How do I use it?
  1. Enter a nickname. If you don't enter one you'll be named Anonymous.
  2. Optionally enter a room ID. If you want to join someone else, their room ID shows in the bottom-right corner.
  3. Be a BonziBUDDY.
Rules
  • No porn, gore, or anything illegal.
  • No spam.
  • Don't be a dick.
  • Don't leak admin passwords.
Commands
  • /name [name] — Change your name
  • /color [color] — Change your BonziBUDDY color (purple, blue, red, green, pink, black, brown, clippy, peedy, troll, and more)
  • /speed [number] — Change voice speed
  • /pitch [number] — Change voice pitch
  • /emote [emote] — Make your BonziBUDDY do something (bow, beat, cool, clap, smile, think, shrug, praise, earth)
  • /image [url] — Submit an image for admin approval
  • /video [url] — Submit a video for admin approval
  • /shuffle — Randomize all Bonzi positions
  • /joke — Tell a joke
  • /fact — Say a random fact
  • /room [roomID] — Switch rooms (leave blank for a random room)
  • /lock — Lock your room (room owner only)
